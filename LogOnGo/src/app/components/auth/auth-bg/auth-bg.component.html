About LogOnGo

LoOnGo is a petrol station management app for:

  • adding
  • viewing
  • updating
  • deleting
log records

Who will use LogOnGo?

Only employees and the administration of Pebo Kenya Ltd are authorized to use the app.

You cannot register for an account if you are none of the above.

Setting things up...
  • Step 1: Register for an employee account.
  • Step 2: Log in to your new account.
  • Step 3: Record the day's logs...
  • or view your profile page.
  • Watch out for announcements on your profile page.
  • Please report any incidents through the platform.
  • You can also contact management in a few clicks.
